Product Description

ShopShare will improve the shopping experience for shoppers all over the globe. ShopShare will allow you to easily and effectively share a shopping list with friends and family with the implementation of Groups. Groups can contain between 2-10 shoppers, and each shopper within the Group will have access to a set of synchronized shopping lists. Once one shopper in the Group checks off an item on the list, the status of the item will be updated for all other shoppers in the Group. ShopShare will be available on both iOS and Android devices.
